\## 4. Maximize Multitasking: Add More RAM  



\*\*Why upgrade RAM?\*\*  

Insufficient memory forces your PC to constantly swap data between the hard drive and RAM, crippling multitasking and slowing applications.



\*\*How to proceed:\*\*  

\- Check your current RAM usage in Task Manager  

\- Upgrade to 8GB or 16GB for smooth performance on most systems
